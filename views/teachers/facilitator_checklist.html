Facilitator Questionnaire

TVET: ABC 1

Go Back

1. How well are the learners/students able to relate their new knowledge to the things they already know?

2. How often do you give learners/students the opportunity to ask questions?

3. How well do your learners/students achieve outcomes set for discussion on a particular unit?

4. How often do your learners/students challenge one another to provide reasons and examples?

5. How often do your learners/students ask relevant and logical question?

6. How regularly do all learners/students participate actively in group work?

7. How satisfied are you with the ease at which learners/students integrate to work in groups to challenge themselves?

8. How regularly do all learners/students finish their tasks within the allocated time frame?

9. How satisfied are you that all learners/students are effective in performing their tasks? If not, why? (write in comments)

10. Are you satisfied with the learners/students capability of performing their tasks on their own? (if not on their own - this would mean they require your assistance)

11. Are you satisfied with the learners/students interest and ability to participate in challenging learning activities?

12. Are you satisfied that the learning materials provided are adequate and appropriate for the tasks assigned?

13. Are you satisfied with the level of "risk" (failure) that learners/student are willing to take in exploring their ideas?

14. Are you satisfied with the number of times that learners/students inform you when they do not understand certain parts of the problem in context?

15. Are you satisfied with the level of proactive actions you take to ensure that learners/students understand the problem in context?

16. Are you satisfied with the level of encouragement you provide to learners/studentas to think on issues or compare ideas so that it can trigger further learning?

17. Are you satisfied that there are enough books, journal and informative material for learners/students?

18. Are you satisfied with the amount / level of tools and equipment that support practical activities of learners/students?

19. How often do you encourage learners/students to make use of career guidance and counselling services? (If the service is not available also mark NO)

20. To what extent do you assist or support learners/students seeking job opportunities within industry?

21. How motivated do you feel working for this Institution? Kindly state the form of motivation the Institution uses in the comments section

22. How satisfied are you with your personal appraisal procedure and outcome? (If you are not appraised also mark NO)

23. How satisfied are you with the resources provided to you to perform your task?

24. To what extent do you plan a schedule for your personal training and development? (In the comments section give some explanation what you plan to do)

25. Are you satisfied with the investment / training the institution has provided to you to enable you to function effectively in your role?
