5. Learning Resources and Trainee Support

TVET: ABC 1

Go Back
  1. On a scale of 1-5, where
    1 – Non-Compliant (Must improve as a key priority),
    2 - Below average (Non-Compliant- has major issues to address.
    3- Average (Compliant has less issues to address),
    4 – compliant level, (just minor issues to address),
    5 – fully compliant, (no issues to address), state the level of compliance by the Institution in each of the following criteria
  2. Verify all Institutional data held on record (Institutional Data Checklist) before commencing this questionnaire
    Take note of any changes
  3. These indicators help to identify whether these facilities are available and fit for purpose.

5.1 a) Does the TP have a library?
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of books, specialized journals and other informational materials

5.1 b) Are there adequate educational or learning materials in the library? (keeping the number of learners using this facility in mind)
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of books, specialized journals and other informational materials

5.1 c) Is the library open at convenient times for learners
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of books, specialized journals and other informational materials

5.2 a) Are the required learning materials for the specific programme(s) being used?
(Indicator) Adequate and appropriate learning materials for learners

5.2 b) Does the learner have a copy of the learning material (Unit Specification without the support notes)?
(Indicator) Adequate and appropriate learning materials for learners

5.3 a) Is there an inventory of tools, equipment and consumables? In REMARKS state date of last update to this list
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of tools, equipment and consumables for specific trades/programme

5.3 b) Are there enough tools, equipment and consumables required for the specific trade? (keeping the number of learners using this facility in mind)
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of tools, equipment and consumables for specific trades/programme

5.3 c) Are the tools and equipment in good and working condition? (check for maintenance / replacements)
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of tools, equipment and consumables for specific trades/programme

5.3 d) Is there a clear and effective repair and maintenance plan (team) for tools, equipment, furniture and infrastructure?
(Indicator) Availability and adequacy of tools, equipment and consumables for specific trades/programme

5.4 a) Is there a career guidance and counselling manual available? In REMARKS state date of last update to this manual
(Indicator) Availability of career guidance and counselling system to support learner

5.4 a) Are the learners provided with the required guidance and counselling pertaining to their career?
(Indicator) Availability of career guidance and counselling system to support learner

5.4 c) Is there evidence that this support system is producing the necessary results?
(Indicator) Availability of career guidance and counselling system to support learner

5.5 a) Are there an adequate number of management staff to support trainees?
(Indicator) Provision of adequate management representative/ trainers as learner support officers

5.5 b) What is the trainer to trainee ratio (trainer: trainee) for both CBT and REGULAR programmes? State these ratios in the REMARKS
(Indicator) Provision of adequate management representative/ trainers as learner support officers

5.6 a) Are there adequate support measures such as scholarship etc. for the learners?
(Indicator) Information about support measures, such as scholarships and extra tuition is available

5.6 b) Are the learners adequately made aware of information about support measures?
(Indicator) Information about support measures, such as scholarships and extra tuition is available

5.7 Are there adequate hostel facilities or other forms of accommodation made available for learners?
(Indicator) Availability of hostel services (*where applicable)

5.8 a) Has the TP collaborated with any industry for networking or to create job opportunities for learners? In REMARKS state date of last collaboration
(Indicator) Evidence of collaboration and agreement with industry for job opportunities for learners.

5.8 b) Does the TP possess a good track record of assisting learners to find jobs through collaboration with industry?
(Indicator) Evidence of collaboration and agreement with industry for job opportunities for learners.

5.9 a) Does the TP have a Policy in place for feedback, appeal and complaints? In REMARKS state date of last revision to the policy
(Indicator) Evidence of a robust procedure in place for feedback, appeal and complaints from learners

5.9 b) Does the TP have a suggestion box, evaluation forms, or record for feedback?
(Indicator) Evidence of a robust procedure in place for feedback, appeal and complaints from learners

5.9 c) Does the system encourage learners to provide feedback, appeals and complaints? In REMARKS state the average responses from learners for the 3 categories since the last visit
(Indicator) Evidence of a robust procedure in place for feedback, appeal and complaints from learners

5.9 d) Is there adequate evidence that the TP has a functioning system in place to manage feedback, appeals and complaints with appropriate outcomes and conclusions
(Indicator) Evidence of a robust procedure in place for feedback, appeal and complaints from learners

5.10 a) Does the TP have a database of past learners?
(Indicator) Existence of past learners/Alumni network connected to the institution

5.10 b) Is there an active and functional past learners/alumni network?
(Indicator) Existence of past learners/Alumni network connected to the institution

5.10 c) Has the TP appointed a member of staff to manage the Alumni network
(Indicator) Existence of past learners/Alumni network connected to the institution

5.10 d) Is the alumni network/year group connected to the institution?
(Indicator) Existence of past learners/Alumni network connected to the institution
